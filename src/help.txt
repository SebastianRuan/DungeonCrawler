    Move
        no: move character north
        ne: move character north east
		ea: move character east
		se: move character south east
		so: move character south
		sw: move character south west
		we: move character north west
		nw: move character north west

    Interact
        u <direction>: pick up item in cell corresponding to direction
            e.g: u no --> use potion to the north of the character
        a <direction>: attack enemy in cell corresponding to direction
            e.g: a sw --> attack the item south west of the character
